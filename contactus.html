Accessibility and Facilities

Our museum is fully accessible to all visitors. We offer:

  • Wheelchair ramps and elevators
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Seating areas throughout the museum
  • Assistance for visitors with disabilities
  • Service animal-friendly environment
  • Quiet spaces for sensory breaks
  • Guided tours available for visitors with special needs

If you have any specific accessibility needs, please contact us in advance so we can assist you.
