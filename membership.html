Membership

At the Community Science Museum, we believe that science belongs to everyone — and when you become a member, you become part of that mission.

Membership is more than just admission to exhibits. It’s a statement that you value curiosity, learning, and imagination. It means you believe that every child should have the chance to ask big questions, make exciting discoveries, and see themselves as future scientists, engineers, artists, and explorers. Your support helps us create meaningful, hands-on experiences that bring science to life for visitors of all ages. It allows us to keep our doors open to school groups, expand our community programs, and maintain the exhibits and spaces that make learning exciting and memorable.

As a member, you’ll be welcomed into a growing community of people who are passionate about knowledge and exploration. You’ll enjoy the rewards of connection — with special invitations, sneak previews, and opportunities to go deeper into the world of science. But most importantly, you’ll know that your contribution helps inspire minds, spark change, and build a stronger, more curious future.

Becoming a member is a simple way to make a lasting impact. It’s a gift to yourself, your family, your community — and to generations of learners to come.

Join us today, and help us keep science alive, accessible, and inspiring for all.

Membership levels

  • Individual Membership: $50/year
  • Family Membership: $100/year
  • Supporter Membership: $250/year
  • Patron Membership: $500/year
  • Benefactor Membership: $1000/year

As a member, you will receive:

  • Unlimited free admission to the museum
  • Discounts on special events and programs
  • Exclusive invitations to member-only events
  • A quarterly newsletter with updates and news from the museum
  • 10% discount at the museum store
  • Reciprocal membership benefits at other museums
  • Priority access to ticketed events
  • Recognition in our annual report
  • Free or discounted admission to special exhibitions
  • Access to members-only previews of new exhibits

Patreon Membership Benefits

As a patreon member, you will receive all the benefits of a regular membership, plus:

  • Personalized tours of the museum
  • Exclusive behind-the-scenes access to exhibits and programs
  • Invitations to special events with museum leadership
  • Recognition on our website and social media channels

High-level memberships are a great way to support the museum while enjoying unique experiences and opportunities. Your contribution helps us continue our mission of providing engaging and educational experiences for all.

Benefactor Membership Benefits

As a benefactor member, you will receive all the benefits of a patron membership, plus:

  • Private events and receptions with museum leadership
  • Exclusive access to special exhibitions and programs
  • Recognition in our annual report and on our website
  • Complimentary tickets to special events
  • Personalized tours of the museum with our curatorial team
  • Priority access to ticketed events and programs
  • Exclusive invitations to donor appreciation events
  • Recognition on our donor wall in the museum
  • Complimentary museum merchandise
  • Access to exclusive behind-the-scenes tours and experiences
  • Personalized communication from our leadership team
  • Recognition in our press releases and media materials
  • Opportunities to meet and engage with our curatorial team
  • Access to exclusive donor-only events and programs
