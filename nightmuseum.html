Night in the Museum – A Sleepover Adventure!

Ever wondered what happens in the museum after dark? Now’s your chance to find out!

Join us for an unforgettable “Night in the Museum” — a special sleepover event for families with children. Bring your sleeping bags and sense of adventure as we transform the museum into a magical nighttime world full of science, stories, and surprises!

🕒 When: Friday-Saturday May 23rd - May 24th

📍 Where: Community Science Museum

🎒 Who: Families with children aged 8-15

What to Expect:

  • A flashlight tour through the museum after closing time 🔦
  • Hands-on science experiments and night-themed activities 🌌
  • A bedtime story under the stars in our planetarium ✨
  • Late-night snacks and a cozy group sleepover among the exhibits 🧃🍪
  • A light breakfast in the morning and a special souvenir to take home ☕🥐

This is a night your kids will never forget — filled with laughter, learning, and just the right amount of mystery.

Tickets:

Spaces are limited, so reserve early!
