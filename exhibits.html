Permanent Exhibits

Evolution

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved our time. You can also take some time to meet a few of the creatures who didn’t survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too.

Biology

From micro-organisms to the human body, major breakthroughs in biology are offering us unique insights into the great wonders of the tiny world.

Library

Come chill, read, and unlock new worlds—no homework required!

Open when the museum is open!

Technology

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.

Accessibility and Facilities

Our museum is fully accessible to all visitors. We offer:

  • Wheelchair ramps and elevators
  • Accessible restrooms
  • Seating areas throughout the museum
  • Assistance for visitors with disabilities
  • Service animal-friendly environment
  • Quiet spaces for sensory breaks
  • Guided tours available for visitors with special needs

If you have any specific accessibility needs, please contact us in advance so we can assist you.

Volunteer at the Museum

Are you passionate about history, art, or culture?
Join our team of dedicated volunteers and help bring the museum experience to life!

As a volunteer, you can:

  • Assist with events and exhibitions
  • Support visitor services and education programs
  • Work behind the scenes with collections and archives
  • No prior experience is needed — just enthusiasm and a willingness to learn.
